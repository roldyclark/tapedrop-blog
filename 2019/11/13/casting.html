Casting @ Tapedrop Live!

Live Sessions
November 13, 2019

https://youtu.be/92dpsW5wYXk

South Jersey Ambient Instrumentalists Casting opened the first Tapedrop Live! last month. Their ethereal compositions set within the 200 year old Cropwell Friends Meeting House made for a spiritual and emotional experience. Enjoy this performance of their song “Engravings” and follow them on Twitter and Instagram.

Harry Herskowitz

Harry is the founder of Tapedrop. He makes films and music under the alias Roldy Clark.
